HOW TO PLAY

Help Mario navigate through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Peach!

Arrow Keys: Move Mario
Space/Up: Jump
Shift: Run/Fire
P: Pause Game

GAMEPLAY TIPS

  • Collect coins to increase your score
  • Jump on enemies to defeat them
  • Hit blocks to find power-ups and coins
  • Power-ups make Mario stronger:
  • 🍄 Mushroom: Makes Mario bigger
  • 🌸 Fire Flower: Shoot fireballs
  • ⭐ Star: Temporary invincibility
  • Reach the flag at the end to complete the level
  • You have 3 lives - don't lose them all!
